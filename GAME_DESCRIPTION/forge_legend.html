Forge Legend

Forge Legend is an immersive computer game that lets you embark on a journey to become a legendary blacksmith. The game takes place in a fantasy world filled with magic and mythical creatures, where you must gather resources, craft weapons and armor, and sell your wares to become the most renowned blacksmith in the land.

In Forge Legend you start with basic tools and materials and gradually progress to more advanced techniques and equipment. As you create more intricate and powerful items, you can sell them for a higher price and earn more reputation and gold.
