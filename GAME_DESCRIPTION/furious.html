Furious

Furious is a fast-paced and action-packed computer game that features a group of highly trained and fiercely intelligent monkeys on a mission to take down a corrupt corporation that is destroying their natural habitat. As the player, you take on the role of the leader of the monkey group, leading them through a series of challenging levels and dangerous missions.

In Furious, you have access to an array of high-tech gadgets and weapons, which you can use to outsmart and defeat your enemies. You must navigate through the jungle and urban environments, taking on dangerous missions and uncovering secrets that will help you bring down the corrupt corporation.
