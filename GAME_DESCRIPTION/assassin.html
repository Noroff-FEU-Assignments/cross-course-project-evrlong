Assassin

Assassin is an intense computer game that puts you in the shoes of a highly skilled assassin. The game takes place in a fictional world of political intrigue and secret societies, where you must complete missions and eliminate targets to advance through the game's story.

In Assassin, you play as a member of a shadowy organization known as the Assassins, who are locked in a never-ending battle against their arch-rivals, the Templars. You must use a variety of stealthy tactics, including parkour, disguise, and assassination, to infiltrate enemy strongholds, gather intelligence, and take out high-profile targets.
